GED

US High School Diploma

The GED (General Educational Development) is a suite of four subject tests that, when passed, certifies the achievement of US high-school academic skills. It is recognized by universities and employers worldwide, making it an ideal pathway for students seeking an internationally respected secondary credential.

Deutsch

German Language Program

GEI's structured German program follows the Common European Framework of Reference (CEFR), taking students from absolute beginner level through upper-intermediate proficiency. Each level is designed to be self-contained, allowing students to enter at their current ability and progress at a steady, well-supported pace.

🇩🇪

German A1.1 — Starter

The very first step into German. Students learn to introduce themselves, count, name everyday objects, and form simple sentences in the present tense.

  • Greetings & Self-Introduction
  • Numbers, Dates & Time
  • Nouns, Articles & Gender
  • Basic Present Tense Verbs
🇩🇪

German A1.2 — Elementary

Expands vocabulary and grammar to cover daily routines, shopping, directions, and simple questions, preparing students for the full A1 certification benchmark.

  • Daily Routines & Family
  • Shopping & Food Vocabulary
  • Modal Verbs & Negation
  • Question Formation
🇩🇪

German B1.1 — Intermediate I

Develops the ability to understand and produce connected text on familiar topics such as travel, work, and current events, with introduction of the past tenses.

  • Perfekt & Präteritum (Past Tenses)
  • Travel, Work & Leisure Topics
  • Subordinate Clauses & Conjunctions
  • Reading Longer Authentic Texts
🇩🇪

German B1.2 — Intermediate II

Strengthens fluency through complex sentence structures, opinion-giving, and writing paragraphs on abstract topics, completing the B1 certificate level.

  • Konjunktiv II (Conditional)
  • Passive Voice Constructions
  • Opinion & Debate Language
  • Formal & Informal Writing
🇩🇪

German B2 — Upper Intermediate

Reaches the upper-intermediate level at which students can understand complex technical and literary texts, express themselves spontaneously, and interact with native speakers with fluency.

  • Complex Grammar & Advanced Syntax
  • Academic & Professional Register
  • Literature & Media Analysis
  • Fluent Spoken & Written Production

About Us

Our Mission, Our People

A movement to make world-class secondary education accessible across Myanmar.

Our Story

Born from a Belief in Every Student

Global Education Institute was founded on a simple conviction: that every secondary school student in Myanmar deserves access to structured, high-quality, bilingual education — regardless of where they live.

We began by producing mathematics textbooks for Grades 10 through 12, carefully typeset with worked examples, exercises, and explanations in both English and Burmese. What started as a curriculum project grew into an institution committed to transforming secondary education across the country.

Today, GEI stands as a bridge between Myanmar's educational traditions and international standards — equipping students not just to pass exams, but to think, reason, and lead.
